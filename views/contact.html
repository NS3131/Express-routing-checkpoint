Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, while complex applications can take 2-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! We offer various support packages including maintenance, updates, and technical support. All projects include a minimum support period, with extended support options available.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with modern web technologies including Node.js, Express, React, Vue.js, MongoDB, PostgreSQL, and various cloud platforms. We choose the best technology stack for your specific needs.

Can you help with existing projects?

Absolutely! We can help improve, maintain, or add new features to existing applications. We're experienced in working with legacy code and modernizing older systems.
